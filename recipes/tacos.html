Tacos Recipe

Description

Tacos are tasty

Here's a simple, delicious taco recipe you can make at home — adaptable for beef, chicken, or vegetarian options.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (or chicken, or beans & veggies)
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 8 small tortillas (corn or flour)
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Diced onions
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • Avocado slices or guacamole
  • Salsa or hot sauce
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Lime wedges

Steps

  1. Prepare your toppings: chop veggies, shred cheese, and set aside.
  2. Cook the filling:
    • For beef: Brown ground beef in a pan and drain excess fat.
    • For chicken: Dice or shred and cook until done.
    • For vegetarian: Sauté bell pepper, then add black beans and corn.
  3. Add taco seasoning and a few tablespoons of water. Stir and simmer for 3–5 minutes.
  4. Warm tortillas in a skillet or microwave with a damp paper towel.
  5. Assemble the tacos: add filling, then toppings as desired.
  6. Garnish with lime juice and fresh cilantro before serving.
