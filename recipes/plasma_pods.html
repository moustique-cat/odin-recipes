A popular snack on the moon of Xenthar Prime, these goo-filled pods burst with flavor and luminescent spice.

Ingredients

    • 4 glo-fruit husks (substitute: hollowed-out passionfruit or kiwi)
    • 1 cup quasar gel (substitute: chia pudding with spirulina)
    • 1/2 cup shredded moon kelp (substitute: kale or spinach, finely chopped)
    • 1/4 tsp antimatter dust (substitute: edible glitter or crushed popping candy)
    • 1 tsp fermented nebula sap (substitute: honey or agave syrup)
    • 1 drop cryo-mint extract (substitute: peppermint extract)

Instructions

  • Slice glo-fruit husks in half and scoop out the insides, leaving sturdy shells.
  • In a mixing pod (or bowl), combine quasar gel, moon kelp, nebula sap, and cryo-mint extract.
  • Let the mixture ferment in stasis (or fridge) for 1 hour until semi-solid.
  • Spoon the glowing mixture into each glo-fruit husk.
  • Sprinkle with antimatter dust just before serving for a cosmic sparkle.
  • Serve chilled with galactic root chips or enjoy solo under a binary sunset.
